About me

Hi there fellow traveler of the cyberspaciousness. I'm Jelle: a programmer and FLOSS enthusiast from the Netherlands. During the day I work at UWKM, during the night I play indie games, make music, code on various projects, watch 80s comedies and read fantasy / sci-fi books.

My fav OS is GNU/Linux, my editor of choice is VIM, my desktop runs Xmonad and I'm really into functional programming (especially Haskell). I'm queer and drink my coffee black.
